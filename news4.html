Founders’ Day Celebration

Date: November 5, 2025

Richmond College proudly celebrated its annual Founders’ Day on November 5, 2025, to honor the legacy of Rev. George Baugh, the visionary founder of the institution. The event was marked with cultural performances, award ceremonies, and inspiring speeches that reflected the values and traditions of the school.


Students showcased their talents in music, dance, and drama, paying tribute to the school’s rich heritage. Special awards were presented to outstanding students and teachers for their contributions to academics, sports, and community service.


The Principal, in his address, emphasized the importance of carrying forward the founder’s vision of excellence in education, discipline, and service to society.
