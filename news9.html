Richmond Leaves Its Mark at Reflections International 2025

Date: August 12, 2025

Richmond College students participated in Reflections International 2025 and made a remarkable impact on the international stage. Their presentations and projects were recognized for creativity, innovation, and academic excellence.


The event brought together students from various countries, and Richmond’s delegation stood out for their professionalism and teamwork. Judges praised the students’ dedication and originality in every category.


The Principal expressed pride in Richmondites, stating that this achievement strengthens the school’s reputation for fostering global-minded leaders and outstanding talent across multiple disciplines.
