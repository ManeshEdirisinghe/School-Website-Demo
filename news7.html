'WANI RASHMI 2023' Concludes in Glamourous Fashion

Date: December 10, 2023

The much-anticipated ‘WANI RASHMI 2023’ grand cultural event concluded in spectacular style at Richmond College. The evening was filled with vibrant performances including traditional dance, music, theatre, and modern art displays.


Students showcased their exceptional talents, captivating the audience with a fusion of creativity and cultural richness. Parents, staff, and distinguished guests praised the high standard of performances and the professionalism shown by the young artistes.


The Principal expressed pride in the students’ dedication, stating that "WANI RASHMI continues to be a platform that nurtures creativity, leadership, and unity." The event ended with a dazzling finale that received a standing ovation from the packed audience.
