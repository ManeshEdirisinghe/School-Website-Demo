Principal's Message

It is with immense pride and joy that I extend this message to the official webpage of Richmond College as its Principal. Leading this illustrious alma mater is both an honour and a privilege. As Richmond stands on the verge of celebrating its 150th anniversary, we proudly reflect on a legacy enriched with academic excellence, co-curricular and extracurricular achievements. Renowned across the nation, the College has nurtured generations of distinguished personalities, including national sportsmen and leaders. I wholeheartedly wish Richmond College continued growth and success, aspiring to remain the most prestigious and inspiring seat of learning in Sri Lanka.

-Mr. R.S.N. Maddumahewagel-
