New Transport Services

Date: September 20, 2025

Richmond College is pleased to announce the introduction of new transport services to ensure safe and convenient travel for students. The initiative includes modern school buses equipped with GPS tracking, first-aid facilities, and trained staff to ensure the highest level of safety and comfort.


These services will cover key areas of Galle and surrounding towns, helping parents manage daily commutes more efficiently. A dedicated transport coordinator has been appointed to oversee scheduling, route planning, and communication with parents.


The Principal highlighted that this service marks another step towards improving student well-being and providing holistic support beyond the classroom.
