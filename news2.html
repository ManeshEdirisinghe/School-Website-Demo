New Science Lab Inaugurated at Richmond College

Date: June 28, 2025

Richmond College proudly inaugurated its state-of-the-art Science Laboratory, designed to provide students with hands-on experience in physics, chemistry, and biology. The facility is equipped with modern instruments, digital microscopes, and interactive learning tools to enhance practical education.


The inauguration ceremony was graced by Hon. Minister of Education and distinguished alumni who contributed generously to the project. The Principal emphasized that the new lab marks a significant step forward in fostering innovation and research-oriented learning.


With this addition, Richmond continues its mission to provide world-class educational opportunities for its students, preparing them for careers in science and technology.
