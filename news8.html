Richmondites Create History at All Island Choir Competitions

Date: March 15, 2025

Richmond College’s choir achieved an unprecedented victory at the All Island Choir Competitions 2025, earning top honors in multiple categories. The students’ dedication, rigorous training, and passion for music were evident in every performance.


The choir performed a diverse repertoire including traditional, classical, and contemporary pieces, impressing judges and the audience alike. This historic win reflects the school’s commitment to nurturing artistic talent alongside academic excellence.


The Principal congratulated the students and music teachers, emphasizing that this achievement showcases Richmondites’ creativity, teamwork, and excellence in cultural activities.
