Parent-Teacher Meeting

Date: October 10, 2025

Richmond College conducted its annual Parent-Teacher Meeting with the participation of parents, teachers, and administrators. The meeting served as a vital platform for discussing students’ academic progress, extracurricular involvement, and overall well-being.


Class teachers and subject teachers interacted with parents individually, addressing academic strengths and areas for improvement. Parents were also briefed on the school’s upcoming events, new academic initiatives, and support programs designed to enhance student performance.


The Principal emphasized the importance of strong communication and collaboration between home and school in guiding students toward holistic growth and success.
