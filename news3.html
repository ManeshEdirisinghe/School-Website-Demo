Annual Sports Meet 2025

Date: February 15, 2025

The Annual Sports Meet 2025 of Richmond College was held with great enthusiasm at the school grounds. The event brought together students, teachers, alumni, and parents to celebrate the spirit of sportsmanship, teamwork, and excellence.


The Chief Guest, Mr. Sunil Perera, National Athletic Coach, inspired students with his speech highlighting the importance of physical fitness and discipline in achieving success. The event featured track and field competitions, relays, and team sports with outstanding performances from all houses.


The overall championship was won by Hill House, while Sea House secured the runner-up position. The Principal congratulated all participants and encouraged them to continue striving for excellence both in academics and extracurricular activities.
