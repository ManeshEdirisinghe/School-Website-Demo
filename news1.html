Richmond College Wins Inter-School Debate Championship

Date: September 15, 2025

Richmond College’s debating team secured first place in the National Inter-School Debate Championship held in Colombo. Competing against 40 schools from across the island, Richmond students impressed the judges with their logical reasoning, persuasive arguments, and confident public speaking skills.


The team, led by Captain Dinuka Perera, debated on topics ranging from technology’s role in education to environmental sustainability. The finals against Ananda College were particularly intense, but Richmond’s well-researched points and teamwork won them the title.


Principal Mr. Wijesinghe praised the team for their dedication and highlighted how extracurricular activities like debating contribute to holistic education at Richmond.
